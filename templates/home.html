our mission

Our mission is to raise awareness and address global issues through fashion and action. At a better tomorrow, we value transparency, action, and the idea that every act of good helps, no matter how small. We address prevalent social issues by relating a charity/organization/unified effort to each release of clothes and donate 51% of profits to the organization. For our current release, we will donate to the Miami Rescue Mission in Miami, FL. First, the money donated would allow the organization to continue to fund and expand their operations to help out their community in South Bend. Also, this will raise awareness so we can lead a more unified effort to help. Finally, we aim to turn the nation away from fast fashion and would like to promote more sustainable clothing options.
