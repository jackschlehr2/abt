about

My name is Jason and I currently am a college sophomore. This past semester, I was unable to go back to campus, so I decided to act on an idea for a brand I have been drawing up for a while called “a better tomorrow.” A better tomorrow was founded when coronavirus first came to the United States. This virus forced schools and businesses across the nation to shut down. This meant that millions of people were left without jobs, and employment numbers hit an all-time high. With these unprecedented times, I decided to take action. With hospitals and individuals running out of personal protective equipment and funds, I realized I had to not only support those on the front lines but everyone affected by the virus. From there, a better tomorrow. was born, and giving back became the central value. With a portion of the profits going directly to help and support others, a better tomorrow. hopes to make fashion not only look good, but feel good as our customers help support others. Please feel free to contact me via email (abtomorrow1@gmail.com) to talk with me about concerns, issues, or ideas!
